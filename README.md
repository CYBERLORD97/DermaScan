#  DermaScan

**AI-Powered Skin Health & Disease Detection Platform**

DermaScan AI is a web-based application that uses artificial intelligence to analyze skin images, detect potential skin conditions, and provide recommended medications with prices and effectiveness. It is designed to make dermatological insights accessible, fast, and affordable especially in regions with limited access to specialists.

##  Problem

Millions of people struggle with:

* Delayed access to dermatologists
* Self-diagnosis using unreliable sources
* Lack of personalized skincare guidance

This often leads to:

* Misdiagnosis
* Worsening conditions
* Unnecessary expenses

---

##  Solution

DermaScan AI acts as an **intelligent skin assistant** that can:

*  Analyze skin images instantly
*  Detect possible skin conditions
*  Provide severity levels & confidence scores
*  Recommend treatments
*  Show up-to-date prices in the Nigerian market

---

##  Impact

DermaScan AI helps:

* Improve early detection of skin diseases
* Reduce healthcare costs
* Increase access to dermatological care
* Provide users with actionable insights
